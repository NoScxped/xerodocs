Xero Commands List

      Faction

      Factions are teams of users who work to earn money and upgrade the Faction. Faction Owners and Faction Admins can invite or remove people.

      Command
      Description
      /faction-create [Name]
      Create a faction (You must not be in a faction to do this)
      /faction-invite [User]
      Invite a user to your Faction
      /faction-kick [User]
      Remove a user from the Faction
      /faction-admin [User]
      Toggle Faction Admin permissions for the specified user
      /faction-stats
      View stats on your Faction
      /faction-store
      View the Faction store

      Economy

      Xero's Economy is comprised of Credits (‚å¨). You can get a job and work every so often to earn more Credits and buy items from the store! If you are in a Faction, 10% of your income will go to the Faction's total Credits.

      Command
      Description
      /jobs
      View the Job list
      /work
      Work to earn money (If you have a job)
      /shop
      View the shop
      /buy [item]
      Buy an item
      /transfer [User] [Amount]
      Transfer your credits to another user
